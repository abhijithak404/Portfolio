DoPack

A seamless courier booking and tracking web experience — designed to make delivery management fast, clear, and effortless.

Context

This project focuses on designing a modern and user-friendly courier booking platform that simplifies the process of sending and tracking shipments. The goal was to create a clean, reliable, and easy-to-navigate interface for customers and admins.

View Project on Figma
Details
Time Frame:

1 week

Role:

UI/UX Designer, Usability Researcher

Involvement:

End-to-end UI/UX design process, prototyping

Tools Used:

Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop

Overview

The Courier Booking Platform enables users to quickly book deliveries, manage sender/receiver details, schedule pickups, estimate delivery costs, and track shipments across multiple modes — air, road, and sea.

The dashboard offers a clear overview of active and past orders, with quick access to key functions like Book Courier, Track Shipment, and Recent Orders.

Challenge

Users often find courier websites cluttered and confusing, especially when handling multiple delivery modes and tracking details. The challenge was to create a clean, intuitive layout that delivers key information at a glance — without overwhelming the user.

  • Interface Prototype
  • Visual Presentation

Solution

The design focused on making the courier process simple and intuitive. The booking flow was divided into clear steps for sender, receiver, and package details, ensuring quick completion. A clean tracking interface was added for real-time shipment updates, while a minimal layout, clear icons, and balanced spacing improved overall usability and visual clarity.

  • Streamlined Booking Flow: Simplified the courier form into logical sections (Sender, Receiver, Package, Schedule) for faster input.
  • Unified Tracking Experience: Designed a minimal tracking interface where users can easily view shipment status and progress.
  • Visual Clarity: Used clear spacing, soft color palettes, and icon-based navigation to improve readability and task focus.

Results

The redesigned interface significantly improved the overall booking and tracking experience. Users could complete courier forms faster and with fewer errors, thanks to the clear step-by-step flow and guided inputs. The organized layout helped reduce confusion, making it easier to review and edit shipment details before submission. Real-time tracking updates also enhanced user confidence by providing transparent delivery progress at every stage.

The clean visual layout and simplified structure improved engagement and confidence, helping users manage shipments effortlessly while maintaining a smooth, consistent experience across the platform.
