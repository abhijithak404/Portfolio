2nd Vahan is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the process of buying and selling used vehicles. The platform ensures transparency, efficiency, and trust in every transaction, offering a smooth digital experience for both individual buyers and authorized dealerships.

Objective

The main goal was to design a solution that enables users to easily browse, manage, and sell vehicles while helping admins and staff maintain control over listings, user data, and performance insights.

My Role & Responsibilities

As the UI/UX Designer, I was responsible for:

  • Designing intuitive and responsive interfaces for Admin Dashboard, Customer App, and Staff App.
  • Conducting user flow mapping to ensure smooth navigation and task completion.
  • Creating wireframes, interactive prototypes, and high-fidelity visual designs in Figma.
  • Ensuring consistent design patterns and a cohesive visual identity across all platforms.
  • Collaborating closely with developers and stakeholders to align design goals with technical feasibility.

Design Approach

I followed a user-centered design process, focusing on clarity, accessibility, and simplicity. The design system was built with a minimal yet engaging color palette, readable typography, and a modular component library for scalability.

  • Admin Dashboard:

    Designed for efficient management of vehicle listings, customer data, and dealer activities with an analytics-driven interface.

  • Customer Mobile App:

    Focused on easy browsing, filtering, and connecting with verified dealerships. Users can view detailed car information, contact dealers, and schedule appointments.

  • Staff Mobile App:

    Optimized for dealership staff to manage listings, update vehicle details, and track inquiries in real-time.

Challenges

  • Balancing multiple user roles with distinct goals while maintaining consistency in design.
  • Ensuring intuitive navigation for first-time users without overwhelming them with information.
  • Creating scalable layouts that adapt seamlessly across mobile and web environments.

Solutions

  • Simplified task flows with clear visual hierarchy and contextual actions.
  • Developed reusable design components to maintain visual harmony across all modules.
  • Introduced guided steps and feedback states for key actions like posting listings or managing leads.

Results

The final design successfully delivered a unified and user-friendly experience. The platformâ€™s intuitive structure enhanced operational efficiency, reduced onboarding time for dealers and staff, and improved customer engagement with clean and trustworthy interfaces.
